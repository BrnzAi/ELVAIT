1. LinkedIn Outbound (Primary)

Connection Request + Sequence Start Here

Target: CIOs, CTOs, COOs, Heads of Digital at ICP companies

Sequence:

  1. Day 0: Connection request (no pitch, just relevant note)
  2. Day 2: Thank for connecting, share relevant content piece
  3. Day 5: Soft pitch ‚Äî mention specific challenge + ELVAIT
  4. Day 10: Value add ‚Äî send case study or framework
  5. Day 15: Direct ask ‚Äî offer free assessment or demo

Sample connection note:

"Hi [Name], I work with [industry] companies navigating automation investments. Saw your background in [specific thing] ‚Äî would love to connect and share some frameworks we've developed for pre-investment clarity."

Volume: 20-30 connection requests/day (stay under LinkedIn limits)

Engage-First Approach Higher Conversion

Before connecting:

  1. Like 2-3 of their recent posts
  2. Leave a thoughtful comment on one post
  3. View their profile (they'll see you)
  4. Wait 2-3 days
  5. Then send connection request

Result: 2-3x higher accept rate because they recognize you
